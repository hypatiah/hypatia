Hypatia of Alexandria

The Mathematician, Astronomer, and Philosopher

"Reserve your right to think, for even to think wrongly is better than to not think at all." - Hypatia

Key Facts about Hypatia of Alexandria:

  • She was the head of the Neoplatonist School in Alexandria
  • She edited Ptolemy's Almagest
  • Author of "The Astronomical Canon"
  • Her father was also a famous mathematician named Theon Alexandricus

Life Events of Hypatia:

Date Event
355 CE Born in Alexandria, Egypt
412 CE The Bishop of Alexandria's Death, which lead to the end of the Library of Alexandria
March 415 CE Died tragically by a mob
